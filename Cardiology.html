Department of Cardiology

Infinity Hospital provides continuum care for the prevention, treatment, recovery & rehabilitation of heart and vascular diseases. We perform the most advanced cardiac surgeries with excellent immediate and long term outcomes. Whether it is a newborn with a complex anomaly of the heart or an adult requiring bypass surgery or valve replacement, our outcomes are on par with the best centers worldwide.
