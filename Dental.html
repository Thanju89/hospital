Department of Dental

At INFINITY Hospital, we have a team of highly qualified and experienced Dental surgeons who provide comprehensive care for a wide range of tooth conditions in adults & children.

Treatment for tooth conditions offered at Infinity Hospital ranges from medication, treatment of underlying conditions.

make appointment

Overview

Dental treatments are carried out by a dental team, which often consists of a dentist and dental auxiliaries (dental assistants, dental hygienists, dental technicians, as well as dental therapists). Most dentists either work in private practices (primary care), dental hospitals or (secondary care) institutions (prisons, armed forces bases, etc.).
The majority of dental treatments are carried out to prevent or treat the two most common oral diseases which are dental caries (tooth decay) and periodontal disease (gum disease or pyorrhea). Common treatments involve the restoration of teeth, extraction or surgical removal of teeth, scaling and root planing, endodontic root canal treatment, and cosmetic dentistry.

Diseases and treatments of Dental

1.Tooth decay (cavities)


Treatment: Fillings, root canals, crowns


2.Gum disease (periodontitis)


Treatment: Scaling and root planing, antibiotics, gum surgery


3.Tooth sensitivity


Treatment: Desensitizing toothpaste, fluoride treatments, fillings


4.Tooth abscess


Treatment: Root canal therapy, antibiotics, drainage of the abscess


5.Oral cancer


Treatment: Surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy.


6.Bad breath (halitosis)


Treatment: Improved oral hygiene, professional cleaning, mouthwash.


7.Tooth discoloration


Treatment: Teeth whitening, veneers, bonding


8.Tooth grinding (bruxism)


Treatment: Mouthguard, stress management techniques
