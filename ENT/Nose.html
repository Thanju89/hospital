Diseases and treatments of nose

1.Allergic rhinitis


Treatment:avoiding triggers, using antihistamines, decongestants, and nasal sprays.


2.Sinusitis


Treatment:antibiotics, nasal decongestants, saline nasal irrigation, and pain relievers.


3.Nasal polyps


Treatment:corticosteroid nasal sprays, surgery, and medications to manage symptoms.


4.Deviated septum


Treatment: nasal decongestants, corticosteroid nasal sprays, and surgery.


5.Nasal fractures


Treatment:pain relief, ice, and sometimes surgery to correct any deformity.


6.Rhinophyma


Treatment:laser therapy and surgical removal of excess tissue.


7.Nasal congestion


Treatment:over-the-counter decongestants, nasal sprays, and humidifiers.
